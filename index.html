PAST TENSES

Words in the shape of فعل

If a three letters word only contains zabr then it means it is a male doing something in the past.

FOR EXAMPLE
Verbs Meaning
He Beated.
He Created.
He Founded.
He Helped.
He opened.



Sometimes a three letters word may also contain 2 zabr and one zer. However, the word remains a past tense.

FOR EXAMPLE
Verbs Meaning
He Listened.
He Thought.
He Praised/Appreciated.
He Drank.
He was sick.



Sometimes a three letters word may also contain 2 zabr and one pesh. This type of verb is mostly used when refering to someone. However, the word remains a past tense.

FOR EXAMPLE
Verbs Meaning
He became Well-Respected.
He became Bad.



PASSIVE VOICE (PAST)

is used to define an action a person did in the past. What if the action happened to him in the past? For that :


Rule 1 - Add a pesh on the first letter of the word, and
Rule 2 - If second letter contains a zabr than make it zer or if it contains a zer than make it zabr.

FOR EXAMPLE
Verbs Meaning
He was Killed.
He was Created. (Only in this case Rule 2 is not applied)

Note that Action is still in the past.
